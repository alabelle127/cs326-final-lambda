What is Study Buddies?

Study Buddies is a study group Our project idea is a study group/partner finder dedicated for UMass students. Users may publicly display their profiles under sections designated to certain classes or majors. Users can set tags on their profile designating what classes they're currently taking, classes they've taken before, potentially subjects they frequently work in/majors and minors, etc. Additionally users may be able to input their course numbers and in return get an auto generated schedule for their classes in google calendar that also takes into account UMass specific special days and holidays. Users can choose to publicly display this calendar on their profile for others to compare their own schedules to in order to find availabilities for their potential partners/groupmates. Additionally users may be able to link an invite to their group or profile to external websites or applications.
